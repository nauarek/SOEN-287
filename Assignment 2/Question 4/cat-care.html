Cat Care


Although Cats are often seen as a more passive pet, often this can be far from reality.

This leads some pet owners to question their ability to take care of their beloved pets.

As a preventative measure we've provided a list of general tips as well as some great links to help pet owners new or experienced on how to care for the feline companion.

  1. Understand their activity level
    • Much like dogs, cats can drastically differ in the amount and frequency of activity thay expect to partake in
    • Some are quite lively and love to play, while other cats prefer to sit in window sills and bask in the sun
  2. How much time should they be spending indoors or outdoors
    • While some cats are huge homebodys, others are borderline street cats that love to parol their neighborhoods
    • Understanding the relationship your cat has with the world around it is helpful in providing it with the best life they can imagine
  3. What kinds of diet best suits them?
  4. How does your cat deal with/interact with other people or animals?

These are only some of the general questions that an owner needs to think about when caring for a cat. For answers to these questions, as well as any further inquiries about the care of these great animals we leave links to some trusted sites below:
